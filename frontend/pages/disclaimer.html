Under the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS), 2023, theft is primarily defined and penalized under Section 303. This section consolidates provisions related to theft that were previously scattered in the IPC.

The punishment structure is as follows:

  • General Theft (Section 303(2)): Imprisonment for a term which may extend to 3 years, or with fine, or with both.
  • Subsequent Conviction: In cases of repeat offenders, the imprisonment may extend to 5 years along with a fine.
  • Snatching (Section 304): A new specific classification often related to theft, punishable with imprisonment up to 3 years.
